FAQ
Got questions?
Here's the truth.
What is an AI detector?+
An AI detector analyzes statistical and linguistic patterns in text to determine the probability that it was generated by an AI model. It looks at sentence entropy, perplexity, burstiness, and vocabulary distribution — signals that differ measurably between human writers and language models. Vex.AI combines multiple detection layers to give you a reliable score even on edited or paraphrased AI text.
How accurate is Vex.AI?+
Our detector achieves 4.5/5 accuracy in student beta testing across 50,000+ samples, cross-validated against GPTZero and Originality.ai. False positive rates on genuine human writing are kept below 4%. For best results, paste at least 150 words — shorter texts have higher variance in any detector. We update our models regularly to stay current with new AI writing tools.
Can it detect ChatGPT and Claude?+
Yes. We detect patterns from ChatGPT (3.5 and 4), GPT-4o, Claude (all versions), Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral, LLaMA, and most major AI writing assistants. Each model has distinct statistical fingerprints. We also detect lightly edited AI text — when someone runs output through paraphrasers like Quillbot. The hardest cases are heavily rewritten AI text mixed with original writing, which we handle better than most tools.
Is using AI always cheating?+
No — and this is an important distinction. Policies vary significantly by institution, course, and even assignment. Many universities explicitly allow AI for brainstorming, outlining, or grammar checking with proper disclosure. Some ban it entirely. Others are still forming policies. Vex.AI helps you understand how your text will be perceived by automated detectors, so you can make an informed decision before you submit.
Is my text stored?+
Your raw text is never sold, shared, or used to train any models — period. If you're logged in, a brief scan record is saved to your personal history so you can track your work. You can delete your history at any time from your account page. Anonymous scans leave no trace at all.
